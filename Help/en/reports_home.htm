REPORTS Help Page

The REPORTS menu is where you can go to do analysis of the data in your Tustena account. It consists of the following submenus:
At-A-Glance
Here you have some preconfigured reports that will show you graphical analysis of your account data. Simply chose which "At-A-Glance" report type you want to see and chose the report options. These reports cannot be exported. 		Reports
On the reports page, you can create customized reports cross-referencing any of the data in your Tustena account. Simply name a new report, then click on the various fields you would like to include in the report. This is particularly convenient for creating mail merge documents for postal mass mailings using Microsoft Word. These reports can then be viewed as HTML, or exported as a variety of file types (.csv, .xml, .pdf, .rtf, .ado recordset).
