ADMIN Help Page

The ADMIN menu helps you set up and manage your Tustena account. It consists of the following submenus:
Import Data
Use this page to import contact information you have exported from another program (such as Microsoft Outlook). An import wizard will guide you through the steps to import contacts, companies or leads. There are also instructions on how to export your pre-existing data from several commonly used programs. 		Users
This is where you can add users and activate or disactivate their accounts. Disactivation is useful if you have an employee who no longer works at your company. You can disactivate his/her account and add a new user without paying for more users since Tustena's fees are based on activated users, not total users. Also, you will retain all of the records and activities made by the disactivated users.
Menu
Choose which menus the users can see or use. You can decide what each group will be able to see in Tustena. EXAMPLE: Group B should not be able to see or use the "Company Information" page of the ADMIN menu. Therefore, do not add Group B to the list of groups that can see the "Company Information" page. 		Account Management
This submenu is where all of the preferences for your individual Tustena account are managed. Go here to change your password, time zone, language etc. Also more advanced features here such as implementing your personal email account in Tustena and deciding which users can view your agenda.
Company
This page is where all of the preferences for your company's Tustena account are managed. Go here to change your company address, phone number, logo etc. There are also more advanced features here such as implementing webservices and VoIP. 		Erased Records
From this menu you can recover erased records.
Configuration
Configure your settings in this menu.
