MAIL CENTER Help Page

The MAIL CENTER is where you manage your email activity. It consists of the following submenus:
Mass Emails
Use this page to send one email to a group of people from your Tustena address books. Use the sophisticated search wizard to chose the parameters of contacts, companies, and lead to which you wish to send the message. An easy-to.use HTML editor lets you create professional emails with embedded links and images. 		Report Mailing Coming Soon...
Send automatically-generated, customized reports from Tustena on a regular basis. You can send reports to yourself or any of your contacts in Tustena daily, weekly, monthly or annually.
Web Mail
View and send your personal mail (POP3) from within Tustena. You can save any of your mails as an activity connected to any company, contact or lead. From here you can also send mails directly to any address in your Tustena address books and create an activity at the same time.
