SALES It's a new Tustena Seccion, from here you will be able to make quotes and place orders.
Quotes
This quotation module is currently in BETA phase (Testing) and it will replace the quotes activities section already present in Tustena CRM.
Quotes can be added, erased or edited. They also can be associated to a Lead, a contact or a company and relate them to more activities. Every line in the quote can be freely added or loaded from the products catalogue. 		Orders
